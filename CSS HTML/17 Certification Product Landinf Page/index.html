Biography

His artistic name comes from the reference of the plains of San Sebastián, where he worked as a child. Initially he decided to call himself Juan Sebastián, for the symbolism of these names: Juan means free, and Sebastián lover. Later, at the suggestion of one of his sisters, he changed the U of Juan to the O and finally, in early 1977, he left with the definitive: Joan Sebastian.

Rey del jaripeo

In addition to composer and singer, Joan Sebastian (also known as El rey del jaripeo and El poeta del pueblo) worked as an arranger, producer and soap opera actor. Among other artists, he produced successfully for Vicente Fernández and Diego Verdaguer.
